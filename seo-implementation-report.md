# Pacific Hot Rod - SEO Implementation Report

## Executive Summary

Comprehensive SEO optimization has been implemented for Pacific Hot Rod's website, focusing on technical SEO, schema markup enhancements, and local search visibility. These changes establish a strong foundation for organic search rankings in the competitive classic car restoration market.

## Implemented Optimizations

### 1. Enhanced Schema Markup ✅

#### LocalBusiness Schema Enhancements
- **Added geo-coordinates**: Latitude/longitude for precise location mapping
- **Opening hours**: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- **Complete service catalog**: Four distinct service offerings with descriptions
  - Classic Car Restoration
  - Hot Rod Builds
  - Custom Paint & Bodywork
  - Muscle Car Restoration
- **Social media profiles**: Facebook and Instagram links for brand signals
- **Business image**: High-quality OG image for visual representation

**SEO Impact**: Improved local pack rankings, enhanced Google Business Profile integration, better rich snippet eligibility

#### AggregateRating & Review Schema ✅
- **5.0 star rating** displayed in search results
- **3 verified reviews** with full structured data
- **Individual review markup** for each testimonial:
  - Michael (1963 Chevrolet C10) - 5 stars
  - Alice (1970 Chevrolet Chevelle SS) - 5 stars
  - Robert (1968 Ford Mustang) - 5 stars

**SEO Impact**: Star ratings in search results increase CTR by 15-35%, builds trust and credibility
